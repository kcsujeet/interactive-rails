## Audit Failure: L39 Concept Foundation Gap

L39 audit initially passed but was correctly caught by user review. The level assumes the player knows what webhooks are, but no earlier level teaches the concept. L38 teaches outbound Stripe API calls. L39 jumps to inbound Stripe callbacks without bridging the gap. The player would not understand:
- What a webhook is
- Why Stripe needs to call back
- When/how the webhook endpoint was set up
- Why the app needs it

**Root cause:** The audit-level skill's narrative reasoning questions asked "how did the player get here?" but never explicitly asked "would the player even know what this concept is?" The check existed implicitly but was easy to skip.

**Fix:** Added Question 2 ("Would the player even know what this concept is?") to both audit-level and design-level skills as a mandatory check with L39 as the case study. Also added to the design-level checklist. Saved feedback memory for future reference.

L39 component still needs a narrative redesign to introduce webhooks before exposing their vulnerabilities.
