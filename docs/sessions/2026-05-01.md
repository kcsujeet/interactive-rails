# Session Log: 2026-05-01

## Phase 0b kickoff: Act 1 reorder (Associations to L4)

Continuing the curriculum reorganization from Phase 0a (Acts 7+8 merge).

Phase 0b is a series of cross-act level moves to fix several misplacements identified during the bird's-eye review:

- L8 Associations: was at the end of Act 1 (after Serializers). Models and their relationships should come before the HTTP layer that renders them. **(Done in this commit.)**
- L11 Callbacks: in Act 2 (Users & Security) where it does not belong; it is a model lifecycle concept. Belongs at the start of Act 3 as the anti-pattern that motivates Service Objects.
- L15 CORS: in Act 2 but it is API plumbing, not security in the same family as auth/authz. Belongs in Act 6 Operations.
- L21 Action Mailer, L22 Background Jobs: in Act 3 (Clean Architecture) but they are infrastructure features, not architecture patterns. Belongs in Act 5 next to Real-Time / External APIs / Webhooks.
- L36 Encryption: in Act 5 (Advanced Features) but it is a security feature. Belongs in Act 2 right after Authentication.
- L40 API Versioning: in Act 5 but it is a release-engineering concern. Belongs in Act 6.
- L43 Soft Deletes: in Act 6 Operations but it is a data modeling pattern. Belongs in Act 5.
